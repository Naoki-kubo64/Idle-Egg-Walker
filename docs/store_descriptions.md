**Short Description - 80 chars max**
Walk & Idle to hatch monster eggs! Sync steps to grow your unique collection.

**Full Description - 4000 chars max**
**Turn your daily steps into an RPG adventure with "Idle Egg Walker"!**

"Idle Egg Walker" is a health-integrated idle RPG where your real-world steps and idle time fuel your monsters' growth.
Make your commute, walk, or run more exciting by raising unique pixel-art monsters!

**ğŸ¥š Key Features:**

- **Walk to Grow:**
  Sync with Google Health Connect to turn your daily steps into Experience Points (EXP). The more you walk, the faster your eggs hatch!

- **Idle System:**
  Busy? No problem! Your monsters continue to gain EXP even when the app is closed. Play at your own pace.

- **Tap to Boost:**
  Want faster progress? Tap the screen to gain instant EXP and speed up evolution.

- **Evolution & Collection:**
  Watch your eggs hatch into babies and evolve into powerful adults.
  Merge duplicate monsters to unlock higher rarities and new forms!
  Complete your collection book with a variety of cute and cool pixel-art creatures.

- **Upgrades & Strategy:**
  Use Gold and Gems to upgrade your Tap Power and Idle Efficiency.
  Maximize your growth to discover the rarest monsters.

This app uses Google Health Connect to securely sync your step count data and reward your physical activity in-game.
